Pizza

Pizza is a type of pasta food.

A dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • 355 ml warm water
  • 1/2 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 500g bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Proof the yeast.
  2. Make and knead the pizza dough.
  3. Let the dough rise.
  4. Preheat the pizza stone.
  5. Divide the dough into 2 balls.
  6. Prep the toppings.
  7. Flatten the dough ball, and stretch out into a round.
  8. Brush the dough top with olive oil.
  9. Sprinkle the pizza peel with cornmeal, put flattened dough on top.
